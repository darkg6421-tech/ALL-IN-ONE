🛂 Passport – Fresh & Renewal

✅ Steps for Fresh Passport

1. Register on Passport Seva: passportindia.gov.in
2. Fill online application.
3. Pay fee and book appointment.
4. Visit PSK with originals.
5. Police verification.
6. Passport dispatched.

📑 Required Documents

  • Identity: Aadhaar, Voter ID, PAN
  • Address: Utility bill
  • DoB: 10th marksheet
