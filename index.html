📌 Aadhaar Update – Step by Step

Last updated: 13 April 2026

✅ Steps (myAadhaar portal)

1. Visit official UIDAI: myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in
2. 'Update Aadhaar' section: Click "Update Demographics Data".
3. Enter Aadhaar & OTP: OTP to registered mobile.
4. Choose fields to update: Name, address, DoB, mobile.
5. Upload documents (list below): Pay ₹50 online.
6. Save SRN: Track update status.
7. Download updated e‑Aadhaar after 7-10 days.

📑 Required Documents

  • Identity: Voter ID, PAN, DL, Passport
  • Address: Utility bill, bank passbook, ration card
  • DoB: 10th marksheet, birth certificate

⚠️ Common Problems

  • OTP issue? Ensure mobile linked with Aadhaar.
  • Upload fail? File <2MB, PDF/JPEG.
  • Track SRN on UIDAI site.
