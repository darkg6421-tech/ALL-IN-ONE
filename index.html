📌 How to Update Aadhaar Card (Step by Step)

Last updated: 13 April 2026

✅ Steps (via myAadhaar portal)

1. Visit the official UIDAI website: myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in (link in official cards above).
2. Select 'Update Aadhaar' section: Click on "Update Demographics Data & Check Status".
3. Enter Aadhaar number & captcha: OTP will be sent to your registered mobile.
4. Choose fields to update: Name, address, date of birth, mobile, email.
5. Upload required documents (see list below): Pay ₹50 online (GST included).
6. Note down the SRN (Service Request Number): Use it to track status.
7. Update completes in 7–10 days: Download updated e‑Aadhaar.

📑 Required Documents (PoI / PoA)

  • Identity proof: Voter ID, PAN card, Driving licence, Passport
  • Address proof: Electricity bill, water bill, bank passbook, ration card
  • Date of birth proof: 10th marksheet, birth certificate
  • For mobile update: No document needed, only biometric at enrolment centre.

⚠️ Common Problems & Solutions

  • OTP not received? Ensure mobile number is linked with Aadhaar. Otherwise visit nearest Aadhaar centre.
  • Document upload fails? File size under 2MB, format PDF/JPEG.
  • Name spelling correction: More than two corrections may require centre visit.
  • Track SRN: Use "Check Aadhaar Update Status" on UIDAI site.
This information is based on official UIDAI guidelines.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

📌 What is the fee for Aadhaar update?
Online demographic update (name/address etc.) costs ₹50 (incl. GST). Biometric update at centre may be ₹100.
📌 Can mobile number be updated online?
No, mobile number update requires biometric verification at an Aadhaar centre.
📌 How to check Aadhaar update status?
Go to myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in and use "Check Aadhaar Update Status" with SRN.
