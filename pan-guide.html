🆔 PAN Card – Apply & Update

Last updated: 14 April 2026

✅ Steps for New PAN

1. NSDL portal: onlineservices.nsdl.com
2. Select 'Application for New PAN'.
3. Fill Form 49A.
4. Upload documents.
5. Pay ₹107 fee.
6. Note Acknowledgement No.
7. PAN delivered in 15-20 days.

📑 Required Documents

  • Identity: Aadhaar, Voter ID, DL, Passport
  • Address: Utility bill, Bank statement
  • DoB: 10th marksheet
