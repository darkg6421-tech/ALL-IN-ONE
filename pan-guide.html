🆔 PAN Card – Apply & Update Guide

Last updated: 13 April 2026

✅ Steps for New PAN (Online)

1. Visit NSDL portal: onlineservices.nsdl.com
2. Select 'Application for New PAN'.
3. Fill Form 49A with details.
4. Upload documents.
5. Pay fee (₹107).
6. Submit and note Acknowledgement No.
7. PAN delivered in 15-20 days.

📑 Required Documents

  • Identity: Aadhaar, Voter ID, DL, Passport
  • Address: Utility bill, Bank statement
  • DoB: 10th marksheet, Birth certificate
  • Passport size photo (scanned)
