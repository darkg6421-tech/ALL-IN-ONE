📌 Aadhaar Update – Step by Step

Last updated: 14 April 2026

✅ Steps (myAadhaar portal)

1. Visit official UIDAI: myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in
2. Click 'Update Aadhaar' > 'Update Demographics Data'.
3. Enter Aadhaar & OTP.
4. Choose fields (name, address, DoB).
5. Upload documents & pay ₹50.
6. Save SRN to track status.
7. Download e-Aadhaar within 7-10 days.

📑 Required Documents

  • Identity: Voter ID, PAN, DL, Passport
  • Address: Utility bill, bank passbook
  • DoB: 10th marksheet, birth certificate
