📌 Aadhaar Update – Complete Step by Step

Last updated: 13 April 2026

✅ Steps (myAadhaar portal)

1. Visit official UIDAI: myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in
2. Click 'Update Aadhaar': Select "Update Demographics Data".
3. Enter Aadhaar number & OTP: OTP sent to registered mobile.
4. Choose fields to update: Name, address, date of birth, mobile, email.
5. Upload required documents: See list below. Pay ₹50 online.
6. Save SRN: Service Request Number to track status.
7. Download updated e‑Aadhaar: Usually within 7-10 days.

📑 Required Documents

  • Identity proof: Voter ID, PAN, Driving Licence, Passport
  • Address proof: Electricity/water bill, bank passbook, ration card
  • Date of birth proof: 10th marksheet, birth certificate

⚠️ Common Problems & Solutions

  • OTP not received? Ensure mobile linked with Aadhaar.
  • Document upload fails? File under 2MB, PDF/JPEG.
  • Track SRN: Use "Check Aadhaar Update Status" on UIDAI site.

Based on official UIDAI guidelines.
